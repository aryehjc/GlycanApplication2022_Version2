• Antheraea pernyi silkworm (APA) contains arylhphorin, a hexameric protein synthesized in the larval body, serving as amino acid storage for growth and development.
  • As displayed on crystal structure 3GWJ of APA, the N196-glycan is important for structural stability of arylphorin storage protein.
  • Analytical ultracentrifugation and guanidinium chloride unfolding experiments revealed... [N196-glycan] is important for stabilizing the hexameric state and overall stability of APA.
